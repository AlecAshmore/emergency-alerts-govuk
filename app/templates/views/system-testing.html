The government and mobile network operators occasionally carry out Emergency Alerts service tests across the UK.

You’ll only get service test alerts if you have an older Android phone or tablet with operator test alerts turned on.

Your device may make a loud siren-like sound for about 10 seconds. You do not need to take action.

Opting out of service test alerts

To opt out, turn off ‘test alerts’ in your settings. If you still get alerts, contact your device manufacturer for help.

For most people, the chance of receiving a service test alert will be very low.
